CHINESE FRIED RICE

Chinese fried rice is a popular stir-fried dish made by putting cooked rice in with oil, vegetables, eggs, and seasonings like soy sauce. It often includes various ingredients such as peas, carrots, and sometimes protien like chicken, shrimp, or pork. The rice is typically cooked a day ahead so it’s firm and doesn’t turn mushy when fried.

RECIPE INFORMATION

The prepartion time for Chinese fried rice is typically 5-10 minutes, which includes chopping vegetables, preparing the rice, and whisking eggs.Then the cooking time is usually another 10-20 minutes.

The normal and standard serving size of Chinese Fried Rice is usually half a cup, but it can vary based on the amount of indiviauls that you are feeding.

The difficult level attached to cooking Chinese Fried Rice is about intermediate.

Nutritional Fact: There are 238 calories in a 1 cup (137.000g) serving size of Chinese fried rice, without meat. The calorie breakdown is 15% fat, 75% carbs, and 9% protein.

INGREDIENTS SECTION

1. Cooked white rice : 4 cups (about 600 g)

2. Vegetable oil : 2 to 3 tablespoons

3. Eggs : 2 large

4. Carrots : Half a cup (about 70 g), diced

5. Green peas : Half a cup (about 70 g)

6. Spring onions (scallions) : 3 to 4 stalks, chopped

7. Garlic : 2 cloves, minced

8. Soy sauce : 2 to 3 tablespoons

9. Oyster sauce (optional) 1 tablespoon

10. Sesame oil : Half a teaspoon

11. Salt : To taste

12. White pepper : A pinch

.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION :

When cooking Chinese fried rice, always try to use cold, day-old rice — fresh rice is too soft and will turn mushy. Keep the heat high, stir quickly, and add soy sauce sparingly so the rice stays dry and flavorful.
